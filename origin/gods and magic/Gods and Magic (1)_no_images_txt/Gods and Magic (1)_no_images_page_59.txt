This metal container looks like a hip flask for carrying alcoholic drinks except it is barely larger than a potion vial. ability, once per day you may drink from the flask to gain a +5 competence bonus on Move Silently, Search, or Sleight of Hand checks. Once per day, you can pour acid from the flask (equivalent in volume to a standard acid flask).
