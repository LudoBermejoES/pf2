Source: [The Inner Sea World Guide](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/The_Inner_Sea_World_Guide "The Inner Sea World Guide"), pg(s). 52

**House Orlovsky** controls northeastern [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") from [Eagle's Watch](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Eagle%27s_Watch "Eagle's Watch") on [Mount Veshka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Mount_Veshka "Mount Veshka"). They try to rise above petty political maneuvers. As staunch allies of the now disappeared [House Rogarvia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Rogarvia "House Rogarvia"), this has landed them in a prickly situation. If Lord [Poul Orlovsky](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Poul_Orlovsky "Poul Orlovsky") chooses to rebel against [Noleski Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Noleski_Surtova "Noleski Surtova"), they could potentially align with [House Garess](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Garess "House Garess") and [House Medvyed](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Medvyed "House Medvyed"). These three houses could disrupt supply and travel routes between [House Surtova's](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Surtova "House Surtova") two power bases, [Port Ice](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Port_Ice "Port Ice") and [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven").

## Heraldry

The house crest is a black eagle with wings spread in front of a gold field. Their motto is "High Above."
