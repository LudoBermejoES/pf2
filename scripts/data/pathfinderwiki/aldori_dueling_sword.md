# Aldori dueling sword

> Fuente: https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aldori_dueling_sword
> Descargado: 2026-04-16

[![](/w/images/thumb/2/22/Orlaelia_Aldori.png/190px-Orlaelia_Aldori.png.webp)](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/File:Orlaelia_Aldori.png)

[Orlaelia Aldori](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Orlaelia_Aldori "Orlaelia Aldori"), an [Aldori swordlord](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aldori_swordlord "Aldori swordlord"), wields an Aldori dueling sword.  
_See also [Category:Images of Aldori dueling swords](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Category:Images_of_Aldori_dueling_swords "Category:Images of Aldori dueling swords")_.

The **Aldori dueling sword** (pronounced AL-door-ee) is a 3- to 3½-foot-long [sword](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Sword "Sword") with a slightly curved blade. Although first developed by weaponsmiths in [Iobaria](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Iobaria "Iobaria"), it is the signature [weapon](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Weapon "Weapon") of the [Aldori swordlords](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aldori_swordlords "Aldori swordlords"), a fighting school from the region of [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland") in the nation of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"). It is only sharpened on the outside edge. A wielder of the Aldori dueling sword relies more on speed than strength to injure their opponents.

Unlike many precision blades, an Aldori dueling sword can also be effectively wielded with two hands, and those trained in its use suffer no penalties to its accuracy.
