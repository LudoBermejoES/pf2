**Lake Reykal** is a grand lake in [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"), 90 miles from east to west and 45 miles from north to south at its widest points. Two great rivers empty into it, the [Awzera](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Awzera_River "Awzera River") from the west flowing from the eastern plains of [Numeria](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Numeria "Numeria") and the lands of [House Garess](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Garess "House Garess"), and the [East Sellen River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/East_Sellen_River "East Sellen River") flowing down from the [Icerime Peaks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_Peaks "Icerime Peaks") and through the [Gronzi Forest](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gronzi_Forest "Gronzi Forest"). The lake also separates [Issia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") and [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland").

[House Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Lebeda "House Lebeda") controls most shipping interests on the lake.

The capital city of [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven") is located on Lake Reykal's southern shores.

To the south, the East Sellen River continues to flow, into the [River Kingdoms](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/River_Kingdoms "River Kingdoms").
