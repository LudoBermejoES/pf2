**Jellicoe Bay** is a small body of water that is part of the [Lake of Mists and Veils](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_of_Mists_and_Veils "Lake of Mists and Veils") in northeastern [Avistan](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Avistan "Avistan"). The bay is the home of the [Jundlan Wrecks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Jundlan_Wrecks "Jundlan Wrecks"), a collection of flotsam left over from a battle between [Issian](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") pirates, now haunted.
