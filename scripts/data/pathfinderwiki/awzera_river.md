Source: [Stolen Land](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Stolen_Land "Stolen Land"), pg(s). 66

The **Awzera River** flows out of the eastern plains of [Numeria](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Numeria "Numeria") in a southeastwardly direction through the southern foothills of the [Golushkin Mountains](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Golushkin_Mountains "Golushkin Mountains") in [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"). Passing through the lands of [House Garess](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Garess "House Garess"), it is joined by the [Gray River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gray_River "Gray River") that cascades down from the town of [Grayhaven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Grayhaven "Grayhaven"), and the [Silver River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Silver_River "Silver River") that joins it just shy of the city of [Silverhall](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Silverhall "Silverhall"). Past Silverhall it empties into [Lake Reykal](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_Reykal "Lake Reykal"). The Awzera River also marks the border between [Issia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") and [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland").
