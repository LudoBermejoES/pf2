The **Little Icerime** is a small river in [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") that has its headwaters in the [Icerime Peaks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_Peaks "Icerime Peaks") north of [Skywatch](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Skywatch "Skywatch"). It flows west where it joins the [Icerime River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_River "Icerime River") as it flows into [Winterbreak Bay](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Winterbreak_Bay "Winterbreak Bay").
