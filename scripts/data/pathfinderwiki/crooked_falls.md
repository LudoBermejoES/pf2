The **Crooked Falls** are a series of five waterfalls, each dropping by 10 to 30 feet, over a one-mile course in the northern [Stolen Lands](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Stolen_Lands "Stolen Lands") section of the [Shrike River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Shrike_River "Shrike River"). Its challenging features prevent river trade between [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") and lands to the south, with most trade traditionally using the [East Sellen River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/East_Sellen_River "East Sellen River") through [Hooktongue Slough](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Hooktongue_Slough "Hooktongue Slough") instead. The [Crooked River](/w/index.php?title=Crooked_River&action=edit&redlink=1 "Crooked River (page does not exist)") flows into the Shrike around half-way through this cascade of waterfalls.
