**House Garess** is based in the western part of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"), in the foothills of the [Golushkin Mountains](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Golushkin_Mountains "Golushkin Mountains"). It is led by Lord [Howlan Garess](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Howlan_Garess "Howlan Garess"), who is a widower. His adoptive heir is [Toval Golka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Toval_Golka "Toval Golka"), a [dwarf](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Dwarf "Dwarf") and son of the clan-chief of the [Golka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Golka "Golka") dwarves' holding. Howlan's own son disappeared in the [Vanishing](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Vanishing "Vanishing") along with all the [Golka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Golka "Golka") dwarves, deep inside dwarven hold of [Golushkin](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Golushkin "Golushkin") within the Golushkin Mountains.

House Garess' crest is that of a snow-capped mountain peak in gray set against a dark blue field. There is a silvery crescent [moon](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Moon "Moon") in the upper right corner, and there is a black hammer across the base of the peak. The Houses' motto is "Strong as the Mountains."

House Garess had a good relationship with the Golka dwarves until the dwarves vanished. Members of the house worked the metal that the dwarves mined.

The House has built several strongholds, [Highdelve](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Highdelve "Highdelve") and [Grayhaven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Grayhaven "Grayhaven"), in the Golushkin Mountains.
