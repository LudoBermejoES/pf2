**Stoneclimb** is a fortress along the eastern side of the [Icerime Peaks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_Peaks "Icerime Peaks") in [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"). It is south of [Skywatch](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Skywatch "Skywatch") and it is in the north of lands controlled by [House Medvyed](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Medvyed "House Medvyed") and from where that House lives and rules.
