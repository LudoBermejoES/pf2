As of [4719 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4719_AR "4719 AR"), **King Noleski Surtova** was [Brevoy's](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") ruler. He has reigned over the divided nation since [4699 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4699_AR "4699 AR"), when [King Urzen Rogarvia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Urzen_Rogarvia "Urzen Rogarvia"), along with all members of [House Rogarvia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Rogarvia "House Rogarvia"), mysteriously disappeared in an event known as the [Vanishing](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Vanishing "Vanishing"). Prior to taking the [Dragonscale Throne](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Dragonscale_Throne "Dragonscale Throne") in [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven"), Surtova ruled [Port Ice](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Port_Ice "Port Ice"), as have members of [House Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Surtova "House Surtova") for more than 1,000 years.

## Titles

King Noleski holds numerous titles, including King of All Brevoy in the Name of [Choral](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Choral_the_Conqueror "Choral the Conqueror"), Lord of [Issia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia"), Prince of [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland"), Suzerain of [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven"), Overlord of [Restov](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Restov "Restov"), Defender of the [Lake of Mists and Veils](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_of_Mists_and_Veils "Lake of Mists and Veils"), Regent of the Dragonscale Throne, Blood of the Conqueror, overlord and rightful king of all Brevoy, King-Regent of Brevoy, and Head of House Surtova.

## Family

King Noleski is a bachelor and has been pressured to marry and produce an heir for years, much to the consternation of his sister, [Natala](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Natala_Surtova "Natala Surtova"), the nation's _de facto_ queen and arguably the more politically influential of the two.

The king has a distant cousin, [Berislav Surtova](/w/index.php?title=Berislav_Surtova&action=edit&redlink=1 "Berislav Surtova (page does not exist)"), who often works as his ambassador, and acts as House Surtova's lead in the Brevic town of [Zmeyka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Zmeyka "Zmeyka").

## Recent history

A prospective alliance between House Surtova and [House Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Lebeda "House Lebeda") that would have symbolically united Rostland ([Elanna Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Elanna_Lebeda "Elanna Lebeda")) and Issia (King Noleski Surtova) and brought much needed stability to fractured Brevoy, fell apart in [4718 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4718_AR "4718 AR") when the king's sister, Natala Surtova, accused [Lord Lander Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lander_Lebeda "Lander Lebeda") of treason. Lord Lander disappeared soon thereafter, leaving the would-be bride to return home in humiliation. Since Lord Lander had been accused of harboring revolutionary sentiments numerous times during his youth, most of the Brevic nobility believed the Surtovan accusations. The notable exception were members of [House Orlovsky](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Orlovsky "House Orlovsky"), who claimed that the facts supporting the purported treason were contradictory and unclear, and countered that this was most likely Surtovan propaganda. This has caused a significant fracture between the houses of Surtova and Orlovsky, one that threatens to escalate into armed confrontation.
