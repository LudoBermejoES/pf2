The **Rostland Plains** are part of the [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland") region, south of [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven") and north of [Restov](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Restov "Restov"). They are the breadbasket of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"). The Plains are also to the east of the [East Sellen River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/East_Sellen_River "East Sellen River") and south of the [Gronzi Forest](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gronzi_Forest "Gronzi Forest").

## Peoples of the Rostland Plains

Their culture is mixed between [Issian](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") and [Taldan](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Taldan "Taldan") ancestry. People in the Plains, though close geographically to New Stetven, still harbor allegiance to the old [Aldori swordlords](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aldori_swordlords "Aldori swordlords") and tend toward opposition against King [Noleski Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Noleski_Surtova "Noleski Surtova").
