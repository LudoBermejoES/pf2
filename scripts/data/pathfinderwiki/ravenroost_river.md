The **Raveroost River** flows from the northwest side of [Mount Veshka](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Mount_Veshka "Mount Veshka") east into the [Choral River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Choral_River "Choral River") in [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy").
