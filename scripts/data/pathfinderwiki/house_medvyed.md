**House Medvyed** is a noble house of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") that holds authority over the eastern lands that border and contain the [Icerime Peaks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_Peaks "Icerime Peaks") and [Gronzi Forest](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gronzi_Forest "Gronzi Forest"). They have maintained the traditions of worshiping nature, the "Old Way". Lord [Gurev Medvyed](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gurev_Medvyed "Gurev Medvyed") heads the [Stoneclimb](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Stoneclimb "Stoneclimb")\-based house.

The people of the area raise mountain [goats](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Goat "Goat") and [sheep](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Sheep "Sheep"). They hunt in the forest and farm what little good land is on the edges of their concerns. Religion in this area tends to be more centralized on [Erastil](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Erastil "Erastil"), but rumors of hidden shrines to [Lamashtu](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lamashtu "Lamashtu") do exist.

The house crest is a black [bear](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Bear "Bear") with black antlers above its head in front of a red field. Their motto is "Endurance Overcomes All."
