Locale

Source: [The Inner Sea World Guide](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/The_Inner_Sea_World_Guide "The Inner Sea World Guide"), pg(s). 52

The **Gray River** flows south from the [Grayhaven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Grayhaven "Grayhaven") area joining with the [Awzera River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Awzera_River "Awzera River") roughly halfway between the [Numeria](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Numeria "Numeria") border and [Lake Reykal](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_Reykal "Lake Reykal").
