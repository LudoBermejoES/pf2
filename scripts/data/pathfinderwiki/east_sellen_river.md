The **East Sellen River** flows south out of the [Icerime Peaks](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Icerime_Peaks "Icerime Peaks") of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy") through the [Gronzi Forest](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Gronzi_Forest "Gronzi Forest"), and empties into [Lake Reykal](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_Reykal "Lake Reykal"). The river continues south past [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven") into the [River Kingdoms](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/River_Kingdoms "River Kingdoms"), passing through the lands of [House Medvyed](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Medvyed "House Medvyed"), [House Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Lebeda "House Lebeda"), and [House Rogarvia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Rogarvia "House Rogarvia"). In the River Kingdoms it flows through [Lake Hooktongue](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_Hooktongue "Lake Hooktongue"), passing the city of [Mivon](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Mivon "Mivon") before being joined by the [Chalkstone River](/w/index.php?title=Chalkstone_River&action=edit&redlink=1 "Chalkstone River (page does not exist)") west of [Liberthane](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Liberthane "Liberthane"). From there it flows south into [Galt](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Galt "Galt") and turns west near the city of [Isarn](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Isarn "Isarn"), flowing through the [Southern Hymbrian Forest](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Southern_Hymbrian_Forest "Southern Hymbrian Forest"), where it is joined by the [Hymbrian River](/w/index.php?title=Hymbrian_River&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hymbrian River (page does not exist)"). It finally flows into [Kallas Lake](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Kallas_Lake "Kallas Lake") where it joins the [Sellen River](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Sellen_River "Sellen River"). Within Brevoy, The East Sellen River also marks part of the border between [Issia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") and [Rostland](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Rostland "Rostland").

[Paizo](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Paizo "Paizo") published a major article about the Sellen River, including the East Sellen River, in _[Dreams of the Yellow King](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Dreams_of_the_Yellow_King "Dreams of the Yellow King")_.
