**House Surtova** is the current [ruling family](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Noble_Families_of_Brevoy "Noble Families of Brevoy") of [Brevoy](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Brevoy "Brevoy"), and the oldest and most influential Brevic noble family. Their original holdings are the environs of [Port Ice](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Port_Ice "Port Ice") in northern [Issia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Issia "Issia") on the shores of the [Lake of Mists and Veils](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_of_Mists_and_Veils "Lake of Mists and Veils"). Their claim to the throne is linked to [Nikos Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Nikos_Surtova "Nikos Surtova") giving the hand of his daughter, [Myrna Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Myrna_Rogarvia "Myrna Rogarvia"), to [Choral the Conqueror](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Choral_the_Conqueror "Choral the Conqueror") in marriage. This marriage allowed the house to keep its power as a staunch ally of [House Rogarvia](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Rogarvia "House Rogarvia"). In [4699 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4699_AR "4699 AR"), during the [Vanishing](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Vanishing "Vanishing"), House Surtova was able to use its high position to immediately claim regency until the Rogarvia's returned.

The Surtovans are known as careful and cunning diplomats. Before Choral the Conqueror invaded, the Surtovans were [pirates](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Pirate "Pirate") and raiders, and the family still has many connections with the pirates and brigands of the region, many of whom are distant relations of the Surtova clan. One of the more active pirates of the [Lake of Mists and Veils](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_of_Mists_and_Veils "Lake of Mists and Veils"), Captain [Vali Dobos](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Vali_Dobos "Vali Dobos"), is rumoured to have a close connection with the Surtovas, although he keeps his lineage hidden.

## Recent history

A prospective alliance between House Surtova and [House Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Lebeda "House Lebeda") that would have symbolically united Rostland ([Elanna Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Elanna_Lebeda "Elanna Lebeda")) and Issia ([King-Regent Noleski Surtova](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Noleski_Surtova "Noleski Surtova")) and brought some much needed stability to fractured Brevoy, fell apart in [4718 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4718_AR "4718 AR") when the king's sister, Natala Surtova, accused [Lord Lander Lebeda](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lander_Lebeda "Lander Lebeda"), head of House Lebeda, of treason. Lord Lander disappeared soon thereafter, leaving the would-be bride to return home in humiliation. Since Lord Lander had been accused of harboring revolutionary sentiments numerous times during his youth, most of the Brevic nobility believed the Surtovan accusations. The notable exception were members of [House Orlovsky](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/House_Orlovsky "House Orlovsky"), who claimed that the facts supporting the purported treason were contradictory and unclear, and countered that this was most likely Surtovan foul play. This has caused a significant fracture between the houses of Surtova and Orlovsky, one that threatens to escalate into armed confrontation.

## Heraldry

The Surtova family motto is "Ours Is the Right," which likely reflects their belief in a right to rulership of Brevoy since their family formerly ruled Issia as a group of crafty pirate-kings. Their heraldric crest is a gray [ship](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Ship "Ship") in front of fields of blue on the lower half, and black with [silver](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Silver "Silver") [stars](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Star "Star") on the upper half.

## Holdings

King-Regent Noleski currently rules in [New Stetven](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/New_Stetven "New Stetven"), but the family's traditional holdings are farther north at [Port Ice](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Port_Ice "Port Ice"), on the shore of the [Lake of Mists and Veils](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Lake_of_Mists_and_Veils "Lake of Mists and Veils").
