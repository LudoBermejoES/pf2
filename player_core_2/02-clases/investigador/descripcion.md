# Investigator

You seek to uncover the truth, doggedly pursuing leads to reveal the plots of devious villains, discover ancient secrets, or unravel other mysteries. Your analytical mind quickly formulates solutions to complicated problems, and your honed senses identify even the most obscure clues. Wielding knowledge as a weapon, you study the creatures and dangers you encounter to exploit their weaknesses.

## During Encounters

- **Combat:** Your keen insights regarding your foes make you more dangerous than your physical strength would suggest. After spending a moment to study your enemies, your perceptiveness allows you to act quickly, striking them where it hurts most.
- **Social:** Few can stand up against your scrutiny. Every conversation is an investigation, after all, and you never know what you might uncover!
- **Exploration:** You look for clues in your environment, serving as a party scout, analyzing puzzles or mysterious phenomena, and pursuing leads.
- **Downtime:** You study up on subjects new and old, make new allies you can share information with, and pursue hobbies that keep your active mind satisfied.

## You Might...

- Start asking questions—including several that are quite involved—immediately after you're presented with a conundrum.
- Strive to uncover the deeper meanings behind anything you encounter.
- Get so involved in a case that you ignore other matters, deeming them trivial.

## Others Probably...

- Find the cascades of information you spout forth extremely helpful, if difficult to fully comprehend.
- Get a bit annoyed that you're such a know-it-all.
- Rely on you to solve mysteries, puzzles, or other challenges requiring intellectual curiosity.

## Class Features

### On the Case

You gain Pursue a Lead and Clue In.

#### Pursue a Lead

**Traits:** Concentrate, Exploration, Investigator

You name one detail you've identified that you think is part of a larger mystery, then spend 1 minute examining it. The GM confirms there's a larger mystery or tells you there's nothing more to learn. If there is a larger mystery, you can open an investigation by defining the question at the heart of the mystery.

**Investigation Bonus:** Whenever you attempt a Perception check or skill check to get closer to answering the question, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus.

**Active Investigations:** You can maintain two active investigations at a time.

#### Clue In · ◇

**Traits:** Concentrate, Investigator

**Frequency:** once per 10 minutes

**Trigger:** Another creature attempts a check that could help get you closer to answering the question at the heart of one of your active investigations.

You share information with the triggering creature. They gain a circumstance bonus to their check equal to your investigation bonus.

### Devise a Stratagem · ◆ or ◇

**Traits:** Concentrate, Fortune, Investigator

**Frequency:** once per round

You assess a foe's weaknesses. Choose a creature you can see. You can Devise a Stratagem as a free action if you're aware that creature could help answer the question at the heart of one of your active investigations. Roll a d20, then choose:

**Attack Stratagem:** If you Strike the chosen creature before the start of your next turn, you must use the result of the d20 roll for your Strike's attack roll. You can add your Intelligence modifier instead of Strength or Dexterity if you Strike with an agile or finesse melee weapon, ranged weapon, or sap.

**Skill Stratagem:** You can't Strike the target until your next turn. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your next Intelligence-, Wisdom-, or Charisma-based skill check or Perception check involving the target (or +1 to your investigation bonus if applicable).

### Strategic Strike

When making a Strike that adds your Intelligence modifier due to Devising a Stratagem, you deal additional 1d6 precision damage. This increases to 2d6 at 5th level, 3d6 at 9th level, 4d6 at 13th level, and 5d6 at 17th level.

## Methodologies

### Alchemical Sciences

Your methodology emphasizes chemical and alchemical analysis.

You're trained in Crafting and gain the Alchemical Crafting skill feat. You start with a standard formula book containing formulas for two common 1st-level alchemical elixirs or tools. Each level, you learn the formula for one common alchemical elixir or tool.

During daily preparations, you can create a number of versatile vials equal to your Intelligence modifier. You gain the Quick Tincture action.

**Quick Tincture · ◆**

**Traits:** Investigator, Manipulate

**Cost:** 1 versatile vial

**Requirements:** You know the formula and are holding/wearing an alchemist's toolkit with a free hand.

You create a single alchemical elixir or tool of your level or lower. It remains potent only until the end of the current turn.

### Empiricism

Everything comes down to data. Calculating statistics and using inductive reasoning allows you to determine the most likely outcome.

You are trained in one Intelligence-based skill of your choice. You gain the That's Odd investigator feat, and the Expeditious Inspection free action.

**Expeditious Inspection · ◇**

**Traits:** Investigator

**Frequency:** once per 10 minutes

You observe and assess your surroundings with great speed. You Recall Knowledge, Seek, or Sense Motive.

### Forensic Medicine

You've learned that criminals tend to leave more evidence on bodies than they'd like.

You're trained in Medicine and gain the Forensic Acumen and Battle Medicine skill feats. When you use Battle Medicine, on a success the target recovers additional Hit Points equal to your level, and the target becomes temporarily immune for only 1 hour, not 1 day.

### Interrogation

People can't help but trust you.

You are trained in Diplomacy. You gain the No Cause for Alarm skill feat. You can Pursue a Lead at the same time you Make an Impression if you ask a related question. You gain the Pointed Question action.

**Pointed Question · ◆**

**Traits:** Auditory, Concentrate, Investigator, Linguistic, Mental

Ask a question of a non-allied creature you can see. Attempt a Diplomacy check against the creature's Will DC.

**Critical Success:** The target must directly answer (not necessarily truthfully). You gain a +4 circumstance bonus to your Perception DC if they Lie. The target is off-guard to your Devise a Stratagem Strike before the end of your turn.

**Success:** As critical success, but the bonus is +2.

**Failure:** The target can refuse to answer.

**Critical Failure:** The target can refuse, and its attitude toward you decreases by one step.

## Higher-Level Class Features

**Keen Recollection (3rd):** Your proficiency bonus to untrained Recall Knowledge checks equals your level instead of +0.

**Skillful Lessons (3rd, and every odd level):** You gain a skill feat for an Intelligence-, Wisdom-, or Charisma-based skill, or for the skill you gained from your methodology.

**Weapon Expertise (5th):** Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons, martial weapons, and unarmed attacks increase to expert.

**Vigilant Senses (7th):** Your proficiency rank in Perception increases to master.

**Weapon Specialization (7th):** You deal 2 additional damage with expert weapons, 3 with master, 4 with legendary.

**Fortitude Expertise (9th):** Your proficiency rank for Fortitude saves increases to expert.

**Investigator Expertise (9th):** Your circumstance bonus from Pursue a Lead increases to +2. Your proficiency rank for your investigator class DC increases to expert.

**Deductive Improvisation (11th):** You can attempt checks requiring trained proficiency untrained, expert checks as trained, and master checks as expert.

**Dogged Will (11th):** Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to master. Success becomes critical success.

**Incredible Senses (13th):** Your proficiency rank for Perception increases to legendary.

**Light Armor Expertise (13th):** Your proficiency ranks for light armor and unarmored defense increase to expert.

**Weapon Mastery (13th):** Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons, martial weapons, and unarmed attacks increase to master.

**Greater Weapon Specialization (15th):** Damage increases to 4 with expert, 6 with master, 8 with legendary.

**Savvy Reflexes (15th):** Your proficiency rank for Reflex saves increases to master. Success becomes critical success.

**Greater Dogged Will (17th):** Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to legendary. Success becomes critical success, critical failure becomes failure. Failures against damaging effects deal half damage.

**Light Armor Mastery (19th):** Your proficiency ranks for light armor and unarmored defense increase to master.

**Master Detective (19th):** Your proficiency rank for your investigator class DC increases to master. When you enter a location with a clue toward one of your active investigations, the GM informs you of its existence and type.
