# Champion

You are an emissary of a deity, a devoted servant who has taken up a weighty mantle, and you devoutly pursue a cause that holds you apart from those around you. You have powerful defenses that you share freely with your allies and innocent bystanders, as well as divine power you use to end the threats your deity opposes.

## During Encounters

- **Combat:** You confront enemies in hand-to-hand combat while carefully positioning yourself to protect your allies.
- **Social:** You are a voice of devoted authority, with strong intentions to embody the behaviors your deity encourages.
- **Exploration:** You overcome barriers both physical and spiritual, providing inspiration to your allies through your actions.
- **Downtime:** You spend much of your time in solemn prayer and contemplation, rigorous training, and fulfilling the edicts of your cause.

## You Might...

- Believe the righteousness of your cause will triumph, no matter how grim the odds.
- Explain how the teachings of your deity apply to decisions and actions in everyday life.
- Be a bit overprotective of your allies.

## Others Probably...

- See you as a symbol of your deity, and carry over their opinions of that deity to you.
- Worry you secretly despise them for not living up to your impossible standard.
- Know that you've sworn divine oaths of service they can trust you to keep.

## Class Features

### Deity

As a champion, you are a warrior in the name of a deity you revere above all others. You become trained in the divine skill listed for your deity.

### Sanctification

Depending on your deity, their sanctification can make you holy or unholy.

**Holy:** You gain the holy trait and add that trait to any Strikes you make. You gain the edict "Do not knowingly harm innocents or fail to prevent harm to an innocent if your direct intervention could save them" and the anathema "Commit murder."

**Unholy:** You gain the unholy trait and add that trait to any Strikes you make. You gain the edict "Do not put another's needs before your own or those of your deity" and the anathema "Commit an entirely altruistic act" and "Put anyone's needs before those of your deity."

### Deific Weapon

You zealously bear your deity's favored weapon. If it's an unarmed attack with a d4 damage die or a simple weapon, increase the damage die by one step. If it's uncommon, you gain access to it; if advanced, treat it as martial for proficiency.

### Champion's Aura

You're surrounded by an aura in a 15-foot emanation. It has the aura and divine traits. This aura is used as the range for your champion's reaction and various other effects.

### Devotion Spells

You gain focus spells called devotion spells. Choose one: *shields of the spirit*, *lay on hands* (if your deity allows heal), or *touch of the void* (if your deity allows harm). You gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point.

### Shield Block

You gain the Shield Block general feat.

## Causes

### Desecration (Unholy)

You always take what pleases you, no matter who it hurts.

**Edicts:** Subvert or corrupt everything in your path that is pure or holy, sow doubt among those holding ideals of purity or holiness.

**Selfish Shield · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy in your champion's aura damages you.

You gain resistance against the triggering damage equal to 2 + half your level. Your Strikes against the triggering creature deal 1 extra spirit damage until the end of your next turn (2 at 9th level, 3 at 16th level).

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** If your Charisma modifier is +3 or greater, your resistance equals your Charisma modifier + half your level.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Each enemy in your aura takes a –1 status penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.

### Grandeur (Holy)

The glowing grandeur of the celestial realms inspires you.

**Edicts:** Provide a shining example for others, enjoy and share the beauty around you, keep yourself tidy and well groomed.

**Anathema:** Despoil yourself by associating with fiends and unholy forces.

**Flash of Grandeur · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy damages your ally, and both are in your champion's aura.

The ally gains resistance to all damage equal to 2 + your level. For 1 round, the attacker is affected by *revealing light*.

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** The enemy takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Each other enemy in your aura is affected by *revealing light* for 1 round.

### Iniquity (Unholy)

You're dishonorable, dishonest, and committed to breaking the false hopes of kindness.

**Edicts:** Destroy what offends you, take advantage of others, cheat, steal.

**Anathema:** Bind yourself with a law other than what your deity requires.

**Destructive Vengeance · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy in your champion's aura damages you.

You increase the damage you take by 1d6, and deal 1d6 spirit damage to the triggering enemy. This damage increases to 2d6 at 5th, 3d6 at 9th, 4d6 at 12th, 5d6 at 16th, and 6d6 at 19th level. Your Strikes deal 2 extra spirit damage until the end of your next turn (4 at 9th level, 6 at 16th level).

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** The enemy takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Each other enemy in your aura takes half the damage you deal to the triggering enemy.

### Justice

You seek justice, following the law and punishing transgressors.

**Edicts:** Follow the law, respect legitimate authorities or leadership.

**Anathema:** Take advantage of another, cheat.

**Retributive Strike · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy damages your ally, and both are in your champion's aura.

The ally gains resistance to all damage equal to 2 + your level. If the enemy is within reach, make a melee Strike against it.

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** If your Strike hits, the target takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Each ally in your aura with the target in their melee reach can spend a reaction to Strike the target with a –5 penalty.

### Liberation

You will see all people free from bondage and prohibitions.

**Edicts:** Oppose slavery and tyranny, fight for others' freedom to make their own decisions.

**Anathema:** Force or threaten someone to act a certain way, engage in slavery or tyranny.

**Liberating Step · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy damages, grabs, or restrains your ally, and both are in your champion's aura.

If the trigger was damage, the ally gains resistance to all damage equal to 2 + your level. The ally can attempt to break free or Escape from effects grabbing, restraining, immobilizing, or paralyzing them, then Step.

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** If the triggering enemy was restraining your ally, it takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** You and all other allies in your aura can also Step.

### Obedience

Society is ordered with good reason, and people should conduct themselves appropriately for their proper place.

**Edicts:** Enforce proper hierarchies, topple illegitimate hierarchies, lead when most suited.

**Anathema:** Let one lesser than you wield power over you or lead you.

**Iron Command · ◇**

**Traits:** Emotion, Mental

**Trigger:** An enemy in your champion's aura damages you.

The enemy must choose: Kneel (drop prone) or Refuse (take 1d6 mental damage, increasing at 5th/9th/12th/16th/19th levels). Your Strikes deal 1 extra spirit damage until the end of your next turn (2 at 9th level, 3 at 16th level).

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** If the enemy refuses, it takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Each other enemy in your aura must also kneel or take minimum mental damage.

### Redemption (Holy)

You make every attempt to redeem those others might slay or dismiss.

**Edicts:** Try to redeem those who commit wicked deeds, show compassion to others.

**Anathema:** Kill a sapient enemy without first offering a chance at redemption.

**Glimpse of Redemption · ◇**

**Trigger:** An enemy damages your ally, and both are in your champion's aura.

The enemy must choose: Repent (the ally is unharmed) or Refuse (the ally gains resistance to all damage equal to 2 + your level, and the enemy becomes enfeebled 2 until the end of its next turn).

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** If the enemy refuses, it takes persistent spirit damage equal to your Charisma modifier.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** If the enemy refuses, you can grant the resistance to yourself and all allies in your aura (reduced by 2 for all).

## Blessing of the Devoted (3rd)

Choose one blessing:

**Blessed Armament:** Select one weapon or handwraps. You gain its critical specialization effect and can grant it a property rune: fearsome, ghost touch, returning, shifting, or vitalizing.

**Blessed Shield:** Your shield gains the reinforcing rune of your level. If it already has one or is a sturdy shield of appropriate level, its Hardness increases by 1.

**Blessed Swiftness:** You gain a +5-foot status bonus to Speed. Allies who trigger reactions while moving in your aura gain a +2 status bonus to defenses against that reaction.

## Key Terms

**Flourish:** You can use only one action with the flourish trait per round.

**Sanctified:** If you are holy or unholy, your sanctified actions and spells gain the same trait.

## Higher-Level Class Features

**Weapon Expertise (5th):** Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons, martial weapons, and unarmed attacks increase to expert.

**Armor Expertise (7th):** Your proficiency ranks for all armor and unarmored defense increase to expert. You gain armor specialization effects for medium and heavy armor.

**Weapon Specialization (7th):** You deal 2 additional damage with expert weapons, 3 with master, 4 with legendary.

**Champion Expertise (9th):** Your proficiency ranks for your champion class DC, spell attack modifier, and spell DC increase to expert.

**Reflex Expertise (9th):** Your proficiency rank for Reflex saves increases to expert.

**Relentless Reaction (9th):** Your champion's reaction gains its relentless reaction benefit.

**Sacred Body (9th):** Your proficiency rank for Fortitude saves increases to master. Success becomes critical success.

**Divine Will (11th):** Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to master. Success becomes critical success.

**Exalted Reaction (11th):** Your champion's reaction gains its exalted reaction benefit.

**Perception Expertise (11th):** Your proficiency rank for Perception increases to expert.

**Armor Mastery (13th):** Your proficiency ranks for all armor and unarmored defense increase to master.

**Weapon Mastery (13th):** Your proficiency ranks for simple weapons, martial weapons, and unarmed attacks increase to master.

**Greater Weapon Specialization (15th):** Damage increases to 4 with expert, 6 with master, 8 with legendary.

**Champion Mastery (17th):** Your proficiency ranks for your champion class DC, spell attack modifier, and spell DC increase to master.

**Legendary Armor (17th):** Your proficiency ranks for all armor and unarmored defense increase to legendary.

**Hero's Defiance (19th):** You gain the *hero's defiance* devotion spell.
