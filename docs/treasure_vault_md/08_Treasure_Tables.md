## You know what the worst part of this job is? Having to keep track of the inventory. Seriously, I have logbooks for my logbooks. Do you know how much treasure a dragon can acquire over the course of a thousand years? Like, SO MUCH! And dragons are terrible at keeping things organized. One day you’ve got all the new treasures neatly sorted and broken down into groups for dispersal into the vaults, then the boss has a bout of insomnia, and suddenly the stacks of neatly organized talismans

## and coins sorted by region and era are just dragon bedding piled in the middle of the floor.
